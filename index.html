What I'm About

I got into programming because I liked making things. That hasn't changed. These days I spend most of my time exploring what's possible with AI - building voice assistants, experimenting with local models, and figuring out how to make AI agents actually useful (and safe) in the real world.

About Me

The person behind the code

I studied Computer Information Systems at Missouri State University and Ozarks Technical Community College. After graduating, I started TrentApps LLC to have a home for the projects I build.

I've always learned best by building things. Right now I'm deep in the AI rabbit hole - there's something exciting about being able to build things that actually understand context and can help people in meaningful ways. Whether it's a voice assistant that can edit your documents or a governance system that keeps AI agents from going off the rails, I'm drawn to problems where AI can make a real difference.

Outside of tech, I like cozy games, sci-fi, and spending time with my dog.
